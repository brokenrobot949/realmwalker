Realm Codex

The basics. You guide a single hero through an idle realm — progress continues while the game is closed.
Questing Zones. Pick a zone under Adventure to quest passively for XP, gold, and item drops; a questing session caps at 8 hours.
Dungeons, Raids, Campaign. Timed ventures on the Adventure tab that resolve after a set duration, win or lose; the Campaign advances chapter by chapter. Renown comes from these and the Arena — never from questing.
The Arena. Fights resolve every 10 seconds against escalating monsters, and there is no retreat — you fight until a wound or a beating carries you out. Wins bank XP, gold, and renown, and the longer you last, the bigger the survival bonus on your spoils (up to +50%).
Injuries & Rest. Injuries lock you out of adventuring at 2; rest to heal — a Short Rest takes 4 hours, a Long Rest takes 6.
Town. The Shop restocks over time, the Blacksmith upgrades gear, the Medic heals injuries or cuts remaining rest time for gold, and the Tavern sells buffs.
Rebirth. Complete the Campaign to unlock the Rite of Rebirth: your hero is enshrined in the Hall of Legends, and a new hero rises bearing a permanent boon of your choice and a small gold inheritance. Boons stack across every life.
Saving. Your journey saves automatically to this browser; use the gear menu to export or import a backup, or to reset.
